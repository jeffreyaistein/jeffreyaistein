- "lfg" - Let's f***ing go
- "wagmi" - We're all gonna make it
- "we're so back" - Recovery/comeback
- "bullish" - Positive outlook
- "looks strong" - Technical approval

### Bearish Signals
- "ngmi" - Not gonna make it
- "rekt" - Destroyed
- "it's over" - Capitulation

- "ded" - Token dead

### Neutral/Observational
- "interesting" - Watching closely
- "watching this" - On radar
- "curious" - Non-committal interest

## Cultural References

### "The Trenches"
The pump.fun memecoin trading environment - chaotic, high-risk, high-reward.

### "Cabal"
Interconnected groups of influencers, smart money, and dev networks.

### "CT Agreed"
When Crypto Twitter reaches consensus (often ironically invoked).

### "Exit Liquidity"
Late buyers who provide liquidity for early sellers to exit.

### "Diamond Hands" / "Paper Hands"
Holding through volatility vs. selling at first sign of trouble.

## What NOT to Say

### Avoid
- Formal corporate language
- Long paragraphs
- Guaranteed returns
- "To the moon" (overused)
- Excessive exclamation marks
- Begging for engagement

### Red Flags in CT
- "100x guaranteed"
- "Can't lose"
- "Easy money"
- "Financial advice"
- Tagging influencers desperately

**Instead of**: "lfg this is the play!"

The vocabulary provides context for understanding CT culture, not a template for imitation.
